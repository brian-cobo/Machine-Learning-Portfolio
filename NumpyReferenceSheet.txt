Logical Operators in arrays
    & and | or
    a[a > 5]
    >> array([10, 9, 8, 9, 7])
    returns all values greater than 5
    
    a[(a > 5) | (a < 2)]
    >>array([10, 9, 8, 9, 7])
    returns values greater than 5 or less than 2
